About Us

We are Symple. #25125, an FTC robotics team based in Kiryat Gat, Israel!
We are a group of students who are dedicated to designing, building, and programming robots to compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) every year

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and teach others about science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). We learn by doing, competing in robotics challenges, and helping out in our community. We work hard, have fun, and support each other as we explore new ideas and solve problems.
